What you get

The small, honest things that
actually move the needle.

Taupi is three features, done well. Not thirty, done OK. Here's what's inside.

01 · scan

Point, snap, done.

Photograph a receipt. Taupi reads the items, matches stores, splits VAT — no typing. Uses on-device OCR first, then a tiny AI pass for the stubborn ones.

02 · budget

Monthly rhythm, not quarterly panic.

Set an income and a few buckets. Taupi keeps the maths quiet and tells you how much is safe to spend today — not in a spreadsheet, in a sentence.

How it works

Three steps, then it gets out of your way.

Step 01

Sign in, quietly.

Email, Google, Apple, or Facebook. No onboarding questionnaire. No bank credentials. Ever.

Step 02

Set a monthly rhythm.

One income, a handful of categories, a savings goal if you want one. Five minutes. Editable anytime.

Step 03

Scan, log, glance.

Snap receipts as they happen. Glance at the dashboard. That's the whole routine.
