# How to delete your Taupi account

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21

Apple and Google require every app with user accounts to publish a public page explaining how a user — even one who has already uninstalled the app — can get their account deleted. This is that page.

## 1. Delete from inside the app (fastest)

1. Open Taupi.
2. Go to **Settings → Profile → Delete Account**.
3. Confirm on the warning sheet. Your account and all associated data are permanently scheduled for deletion.

## 2. If you have uninstalled the app

Email **rainerslovkins@gmail.com** from the email address associated with your Taupi account, with the subject **"Delete my account"**. Include nothing else in the body (so we can automate verification).

We reply within **7 working days** to confirm deletion.

## 3. What gets deleted

- Your account and login credentials.
- All expenses, goals, investments, profile signals, and receipt metadata.
- Your push notification token.
- Customer-support correspondence linked to your account (after 24 months, per our privacy policy).

## 4. What stays (and why)

- **Anonymous community price data** (only if you previously opted in to price sharing). These rows — store, item, price, date — are never linked to your user ID, so they remain in the shared catalogue and cannot be reassembled into your identity.
- **Database backups** rotate out within **90 days** of deletion.
- **Server logs** (IP, request metadata) keep for up to **14 days** and then rotate out.
