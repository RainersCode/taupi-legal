Scan

A receipt takes six seconds — not sixty.

On-device OCR handles most of the work. A tiny AI pass cleans the stubborn lines. Items, store, VAT and date are split automatically.

  • On-device first. Most receipts never leave your phone.
  • Edit inline. Swipe a line item to remove or recategorise.
  • Privacy by design. Receipt images are discarded after parsing.

Budget

Monthly rhythm, not quarterly panic.

Set your income once. Pick a handful of buckets. Taupi computes a single number — how much is safe to spend today — and keeps the rest out of your way.

  • One number. "Safe to spend today" — no mental arithmetic.
  • Categories without clutter. As few as 3, as many as you need.
  • Drop-in adjustments. Change income or buckets any time, without reset.

Goals

Long horizons, gently tracked.

Set a target and a date. Taupi computes the small monthly number that gets you there. Share any goal with a partner, or keep it private.

  • Pace-aware. Ahead, on-track, or behind — updated daily.
  • Shared if you want. Two people, one goal, split contributions.
  • Soft-delete. Paused goals can be brought back later.
Security & privacy

Your data stays yours. By default, and by design.

Taupi never connects to your bank. We collect the minimum needed to run the app, store it in the EU under Row-Level Security, and give you one-tap deletion whenever you want out.

EU-hosted (Supabase · Frankfurt) Row-Level Security TLS 1.3 end-to-end No tracking pixels GDPR-compliant
How it works

Three steps, then it gets out of your way.

1

Sign in, quietly.

Email, Apple, Google or Facebook. No onboarding questionnaire. No bank credentials.

2

Set your monthly rhythm.

Income, a few categories, one savings goal if you want one. Five minutes, editable anytime.

3

Scan, log, glance.

Snap receipts as they happen. Glance at the daily number. That's the whole routine.
